How to Join Us

To join AcademiaOnline and access our online tuition platform, follow these simple steps:


  1. Visit our website and navigate to the "Contact Us" section.
  2. Fill out the registration form with your personal details.
  3. The officials will contact you.
  4. You can discuss and select the desired class and payment method.
  5. Receive a confirmation email with your login details.
  6. Log in to your account and set up your profile.
  7. Access the course materials and start learning.
  8. Attend live sessions and interact with your tutors.
  9. Complete assignments and participate in discussions.
  10. Get feedback and track your progress through regular assessments.

If you encounter any issues during the registration process, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
